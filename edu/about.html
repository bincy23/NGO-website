About Us

Thankam Social Awareness Foundation Trust is for the purpose of carrying out the objects hereinafter set out without any distinction of caste, color or creed. It has no profit motive. With the sole aim of involving in social charitable, religious, promotional, educational and cultural.

Our Aim
  • To create awareness about the government schemes and policies of developmental programs to the society.
  • To provide educational guidance for the needy.
  • To assist students and graduates to avail job opportunities.
  • To provide guidance to the public to get the government certificates, etc,..
  • To conduct vocational training programs.
